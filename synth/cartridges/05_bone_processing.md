## 1. Почему scapula

Бычья **лопатка** (scapula) выбрана как basis для bone cartridge по нескольким причинам:

- **Geometry**: естественно плоская, broad blade ~150×200мм с толщиной 4–8мм в основной части. Из одной scapula можно нарезать **1–2 пластины** 100×40×4мм без значительных отходов.
- **Density**: cortical bone (плотная наружная) однородна в blade region. Отлично резонирует.
- **Availability**: byproduct мясной промышленности, дёшево.
- **Ethics**: food industry waste — никаких specific убийств для craft. Documenting source важно для boutique brand.

**Альтернативы** (если scapula недоступна):
- **Femur** (бедренная) — толстая, цилиндрическая. Cortical wall 6–10мм. Можно вырезать slab, но геометрия хуже — плоская поверхность ограничена кривизной.
- **Rib** (рёбра) — изогнутые, узкие. Не годится для 40мм ширины.
- **Pelvis** (тазовая) — есть плоские участки, но менее доступна и грязнее в обработке.

**Рекомендация**: scapula. Если нужно выйти за рамки cattle — **buffalo / bison scapula** (крупнее, толще, premium SKU). **Mammoth ivory** — luxury limited edition, $400–600 retail (выходит за scope этого doc).

## 2. Sourcing — где взять

### Option A — Local butcher / soup bones

**Самый дешёвый и быстрый старт**.

- Звоните в local мясной магазин или butcher shop.
- Просите "soup bones" / "stock bones" / "marrow bones" от **beef supplier** (не свинина — свиная кость мягче, иначе ритуальный смысл).
- Specifically просите **scapula** ("blade bone", "shoulder blade").
- Цена: **$3–5/кг raw**. Одна scapula весит ~1.5–2кг.
- Обычно требуется **заказ заранее** (1–3 дня) — большинство butchers не держит scapula в обычном stock'е.

**Что получите**: raw bone с прикреплёнными остатками мяса, fat, периост (connective tissue membrane), хрящи на joint area. Полная обработка нужна.

### Option B — Pet store raw bones

Многие pet магазины продают beef femur "raw bones" для собак, обычно уже **partially cleaned** (мясо снято, частично degreased).

- Цена: $5–15 за bone.
- Минус: чаще femur, не scapula. Надо проверять.
- Плюс: economy на cleaning (на 1–2 недели меньше maceration).

### Option C — Craft suppliers (рекомендуется для production)

Готовые cleaned/degreased bones:
- **Boyer's Bones** (US) — wholesale natural bones, scapula от $10–15.
- **Skulls Unlimited International** (US) — taxidermy/craft, premium quality.
- **Northwest Bone Carving Supply** (US) — specifically для bone craft.
- **eBay search**: "bovine scapula craft" — часто $15–25 per bone, cleaned.
- **Etsy**: artisan suppliers, $20–40, often premium-treated.

**Цена**: $10–30 за clean scapula. Saves 3–4 weeks обработки.

### Option D — Slaughterhouse direct (production scale)

Для batch production 50+ cartridges:
- Прямой контакт с meat processing facility.
- Цена: $1/кг raw, иногда бесплатно (waste product).
- **Требует permit** в некоторых юрисдикциях для regular pickup.
- Полная обработка с нуля (3–4 недели elapsed).

### Ethical / legal notes

**Cattle bone** (Bos taurus) — **legal everywhere** как food byproduct. Документировать source (butcher receipt) для transparency.

**Запрещённые материалы** (НЕ использовать):
- **Ivory** (любая) — CITES restricted, illegal в большинстве стран.
- **Human bone** — обвиняемо законом, очевидно.
- **Endangered species** (тигр, носорог, etc.) — CITES.
- **Wild-collected old bones** без provenance — могут быть archaeological violations в зависимости от региона.

**Recommend**: source receipt и photo от butcher, keep with cartridge serial registry. Boutique brand transparency.

## 3. Initial cleaning (defleshing)

Цель: убрать мясо, fat layer, периост, хрящи. Получить голую кость.

### Method A — Boiling (быстро, ~1 day)

**Pros**: быстро.
**Cons**: thermal stress на кость → возможны микро-cracks, пожелтение от fat.

**Procedure**:
1. Большая кастрюля (5L+), воды покрыть кость на 5см.
2. Добавить **1 столовую ложку Dawn dish soap** (или эквивалент с degreasing properties) — помогает emulsify fat.
3. **Simmer (НЕ boil)**: 85–90°C, 6–8 часов. Не кипятить — boiling crackает кость.
4. **Replace water каждые 2 часа** — ставшая жирной/мутной → свежая горячая.
5. После: knife / spatula + wire brush — отскребать мясо. Поэтому жидкое hot вода смягчает.
6. Final rinse cold water.

**Tools**: boil pot (dedicate one — никогда не использовать для food after), tongs, scrub brush.

### Method B — Cold maceration (медленно, 1–4 weeks, лучшее качество)

**Pros**: бережно, без thermal damage. Bacteria разлагают tissue полностью.
**Cons**: LONG time, FOUL smell.

**Procedure**:
1. Bucket с lid (5L+), вне дома (балкон, гараж, навес).
2. Bone + warm tap water до полного покрытия. Lid not airtight (gases должны escape, или bucket explodes).
3. Wait. **NOT add anything** — natural bacteria от bone surface ferment tissue.
4. 1–2 week mark: liquid mутный, ужасный smell. Tissue распадается.
5. **Refresh water каждую неделю**. Wear N95 + nitrile gloves для handling.
6. После 3–4 weeks: tissue полностью отвалилось от bone. Final rinse, scrub.

**Smell control**:
- Bucket с tightly fitted lid + plastic film tape.
- Use оutdoor space только.
- Have neighbours warned (no joke — smell carries).

### Method C — Pressure cooker (компромисс, 1–2 hours)

**Pros**: faster than boil, less thermal damage than open simmer.
**Cons**: limited capacity, requires equipment.

**Procedure**:
1. Pressure cooker, water + dish soap.
2. Bone inside, pressurize.
3. Cook 1–2 hours at full pressure.
4. Cool, release, scrape tissue off.

### Method D — Dermestid beetles (профессиональный, museum-grade)

Beetles едят tissue без damage to bone. Используется в музеях.

- Setup: terrarium с beetle colony.
- 1–3 days per bone, perfectly cleaned.
- **Not practical для DIY** — beetles требуют permanent care.

### Recommendation

- **Single cartridge prototype**: Method A (boil 1 day).
- **Production batch (10+ cartridges)**: Method B (maceration outdoor 3 weeks). Better quality, scales.
- **Premium SKU (titanium / mammoth tier)**: Method D considered.

## 4. Degreasing — обезжиривание

После defleshing кость **всё ещё содержит fat внутри** (cancellous structure, marrow remnants). Без degreasing — желтеет со временем, smell, soft acoustic character.

### Why important

Bone fat continues to seep after defleshing. На finished polished cartridge это:
- **Yellowing** через 6–12 месяцев → looks неприятно.
- **Musky smell** → unpleasant during performance.
- **Acoustic dampening** — fat кости softens vibration, RT60 reduced.

### Method A — Ammonia soak (стандарт, 1–2 weeks)

**Pros**: эффективно, дёшево.
**Cons**: ammonia smell, requires ventilation.

**Procedure**:
1. Large bucket, **household ammonia** (sudsy ammonia from cleaning aisle). Diluted **1:10** (1 part ammonia : 10 parts water).
2. Bone fully submerged.
3. **Outside or VERY ventilated space** (open garage). Ammonia fumes irritating.
4. Wait **7–14 days**. Liquid становится yellow/brown — это extracted fat.
5. **Refresh каждые 3–4 days** — liquid saturated с fat, перестаёт работать.
6. Final rinse cold water тщательно.

**PPE**: nitrile gloves, eye protection, respirator с ammonia cartridge (NIOSH P100 + organic vapor).

### Method B — Sodium carbonate (washing soda, медленнее)

**Pros**: less harsh smell, более safe.
**Cons**: 4–6 weeks для полного degreasing.

**Procedure**:
- Washing soda 1:20 в воде.
- Soak 4–6 weeks, refresh weekly.
- Less aggressive but more thorough.

### Method C — Acetone soak (production-grade, 1 week)

**Pros**: fastest, most thorough.
**Cons**: flammable, expensive, requires sealed container.

**Procedure**:
- Glass jar (acetone dissolves plastic).
- Acetone industrial grade, full submersion.
- Sealed lid.
- 5–7 days.
- Air dry в ventilated area 24h.

**Хранение acetone** — flammable cabinet, FAR от ignition sources.

### Method D — Heat degreasing (industrial)

- Heated tank 50–60°C с water + degreasing detergent.
- 5–7 days.
- Used by taxidermy professionals.
- Requires dedicated equipment.

### Recommendation

- **DIY single**: Method A (ammonia, 2 weeks outdoor).
- **Production**: Method C (acetone, 1 week) для consistency.

### How to know it's done

Hold bone up to light. **Properly degreased bone**:
- White или off-white (not yellow).
- Slight translucency around edges.
- No greasy feel to touch.
- No smell when warm.

If still yellow/translucent yellow → not done, продолжить degreasing.

## 5. Whitening — отбеливание

**ВАЖНО**: для bleach НЕ используем хлорный отбеливатель (chlorine bleach / NaOCl). Он destroys bone protein structure → bone становится chalky, brittle, теряет acoustic properties.

Только **hydrogen peroxide** (H₂O₂).

### Method A — Hydrogen peroxide soak (стандарт)

**Concentrations**:
- **3% H₂O₂** (drugstore aisle, hair peroxide): мягко, требует 1–2 weeks.
- **6% H₂O₂** (salon supply): средняя сила, 5–7 days.
- **12% H₂O₂** (industrial / cosmetic supply): сильнее, 2–4 days. **Recommended**.
- **35% H₂O₂** (food-grade industrial): очень агрессивно, 8–24 hours. Требует dilution.

**Procedure (12%)**:
1. Glass или **HDPE plastic** контейнер (peroxide reacts с metals — никаких metal containers).
2. Peroxide до полного покрытия bone.
3. **Cover loosely** (gases escape — peroxide decomposes к O₂ + H₂O).
4. Soak 2–4 days, **room temperature** (warm accelerates но может damage).
5. Check daily — visible bubbling normally, bone whitening visible через 24h.
6. **Bone должна стать "ivory-white"** — not stark white (chlorine effect — looks fake).
7. Rinse cold water, air dry.

**Repeat** если first cycle insufficient.

### Method B — Sun bleaching (natural, 2–4 weeks)

**Pros**: free, no chemicals, naturally whitens.
**Cons**: requires sunlight, weather-dependent.

**Procedure**:
- Place degreased bone outdoors на sun-facing surface.
- Rotate every 2–3 days.
- 2–4 weeks для full whitening.
- **Result**: more matte off-white finish — "ritual aged" look.

Often combined: peroxide bleach (1 cycle) → sun finish (1 week) для balanced character.

### Aesthetic considerations

**Stark white** (peroxide max):
- Looks "modern", clinical.
- Suits crisp branding / contrast против wood/dark cartridge.

**Natural ivory** (peroxide minimal или sun-only):
- Warm off-white, organic character.
- Suits postapocalypse/ritual brand identity лучше.
- **Recommended для Last Night cartridge** — match concept "Body Blood And Salt / Bones Dust".

**Aged yellow** (no whitening):
- Skip step entirely если хотим vintage character.
- Some buyers prefer this.
- Risk: continued yellowing если degreasing incomplete.

### Recommendation для Last Night cartridge

Single light peroxide cycle (12%, 24h) для consistency, без полного отбеливания. Сохранить natural ivory tone — matches series aesthetic.

## 6. Drying — сушка

Bone после wet processing содержит significant water в cancellous structure. Must dry **полностью** перед cutting и polishing — иначе:

- Bone shrinks 2–5% при drying — cartridge dimensions сместятся.
- Cracks при stress (drying или mechanical).
- Acoustic properties unstable до full dry.

### Drying methods

**Method A — Air dry (slow, safe, рекомендовано)**:
- Warm dry room, не direct heat.
- Place на rack (airflow вокруг).
- Humidity <50% ideal.
- **1–2 weeks** для full dry.
- Check periodically — flip orientation для even drying.

**Method B — Oven low temp (faster)**:
- Oven 50–60°C maximum (НЕ выше — protein denatures, bone cracks).
- Door slightly ajar для humidity escape.
- 12–24 hours.
- Risk: heat stress если non-uniform.

**Method C — Silica gel desiccator (controlled)**:
- Sealed container с silica gel beads.
- 5–7 days, replace beads when saturated (color change indicators).
- Best для consistent humidity removal.
- Used by professionals.

### How to know it's dry

- **Weight stable**: weigh daily, when 3 consecutive days show same weight ±0.5g — dry.
- **Visual**: surface chalky, no sheen.
- **Sound**: tap with knuckle — sharp "tick" не dull "thunk".
- **Hygrometer test**: place в sealed container с hygrometer 24h. Если humidity rises → bone still wet.

### Critical: oversize cutting

Bone shrinks during drying. **DO NOT cut to final dimensions wet**.

- Plan cartridge plate: 100×40×4мм final.
- Cut wet OVERSIZE: **104×42×5мм** (gives 4% margin).
- Allow full drying.
- Final-trim к 100×40×4мм after dry.

This adds workflow step но prevents undersized parts.

## 7. Cutting — резка пластины

Cattle scapula имеет broad blade (lamina) part, ~150×200мм. From this можно вырезать **1–2 plate blanks** 104×42мм.

### Anatomy refresher

Scapula structure:
- **Spine** (ridge along center) — thick raised ridge, bypass.
- **Supraspinous fossa** (выше spine) — thinner part, ~3–5мм.
- **Infraspinous fossa** (ниже spine) — main blade area, ~4–8мм. **Best material**.
- **Glenoid cavity** (joint end) — porous, dense bone, не годится для plate.
- **Coracoid process** — projecting bone, не годится.

**Best cutting area**: middle of infraspinous fossa, parallel к spine, avoiding ridge.

### Cutting tools

| Tool | Pros | Cons | Cost |
|------|------|------|------|
| **Wet tile saw** (рекомендовано) | Clean cuts, water cooled, no dust | Bulky, needs water source | $80–150 |
| Bandsaw + wood blade 14 TPI | Fast, accurate straight cuts | Dust, requires good ventilation | $300+ |
| Jigsaw + bi-metal blade | Affordable, accessible | Wandering cuts, slow | $50 |
| Hacksaw (manual) | Cheap, no power | Slow, exhausting, less accurate | $15 |
| Dremel + cutting wheel | Fine work, curves | Limited depth, slow | $80 |
| Diamond wire saw | Precision, minimal kerf | Slow, expensive | $200+ |

### Recommendation

**Wet tile saw** (Diamond Pacific или RYOBI tile cutter) с water cooling — best balance:
- Bone is hardness similar to soft ceramic, диск по тилу cuts через bone smoothly.
- Water cooling prevents heat (heat denatures protein, releases noxious smoke).
- Минимум dust.

### Cutting procedure

1. **Plan cuts на bone** — pencil markings show plate outlines (104×42мм rectangles), avoid spine, joint, edges.
2. **Secure scapula** в clamp or vise — bone не должна shift.
3. **Wet tile saw setup**: water reservoir full, blade clean.
4. **Slow feed**: ~10мм/sec, не force.
5. **First cut**: outline plate (4 cuts → rectangle). Each cut ~1–2 минуты.
6. **Trim к flat thickness** (5мм target wet, 4мм final dry):
   - Если scapula thicker — second cut parallel к first surface для thickness control.
   - Или sand down к thickness в next step.
7. **Edge clean-up**: deburr cut edges с file.

### Yield optimization

From single scapula:
- 1 large plate (104×42мм) typically — limited by spine ridge avoidance.
- Sometimes 2 если scapula большая (premium cattle).
- Smaller off-cuts (50×30мм) можно использовать для test pieces / quality control.

### Cutting safety

- **N95 mask + face shield** — bone dust contains calcium phosphate aerosols, allergenic.
- **Wet cutting only** — dry bone cutting → fine dust airborne.
- **Eye protection** — bone chips can fly при saw kickback.
- **Gloves** — sharp edges.
- **Ventilation** — even wet cutting produces some aerosol.

## 8. Sanding / polishing

Bone polishes beautifully — finished plate можно довести до **mirror ivory finish**. Полная progression sandpaper grits → polishing compound.

### Goal

Remove saw marks, flatten к thickness 4мм exactly, smooth surface, achieve **soft sheen** finish (not stark mirror — overly polished bone looks plastic, плохо matches concept).

### Stages

**Stage 1 — Rough flatten (80-grit)**:
- Belt sander или orbital sander с 80-grit aluminum oxide.
- **Goal**: remove saw marks, flatten surfaces к target thickness 4мм.
- Wet sanding preferred (water spray) — reduces dust, prevents heat.
- **Check thickness** с calipers каждые 30 секунд — bone removes faster than wood.
- Flat surface check: place on flat granite slab — should not rock.

**Stage 2 — Smooth (120-grit)**:
- Same sander, 120-grit.
- Removes 80-grit scratches.
- Visible improvement в smoothness.

**Stage 3 — Pre-polish (220-grit)**:
- Hand sanding с 220-grit на flat backing block.
- Move в circular pattern, no single-direction strokes (creates linear scratches).

**Stage 4 — Fine smooth (400-grit, 600-grit)**:
- 400 → 600 sequential.
- Wet sanding с water — slurry helps lift dust + provides cooling.
- Surface starts looking smooth, edges crisp.

**Stage 5 — Pre-mirror (800–1200-grit)**:
- 800 → 1000 → 1200 sequential.
- Wet sanding mandatory — dry sanding generates heat, can crack thin bone plate.
- Visible sheen developing.

**Stage 6 — Polish (compound)**:

Two paths — mirror finish или soft sheen:

**Path A — Mirror finish** (high-gloss, "modern" look):
- 2000-grit wet sanding.
- Buffing wheel + **white rouge** или **Tripoli compound**:
  - Bench grinder с buffing wheel attached.
  - Apply polish stick к wheel.
  - Press bone plate firmly, sweep back-and-forth.
  - **Heat warning**: friction generates heat, periodically cool plate в water.
- Final: **carnauba wax** на soft cloth, hand-buff к final shine.
- Result: shines like ivory, very smooth.

**Path B — Soft sheen** (рекомендовано для Last Night, postapocalypse character):
- Stop sandpaper at 1000-grit.
- Hand-rub с **bone burnishing tool** (deer antler tip, smooth river stone, или actual bone fragment).
- Pressure + friction creates "burnished sheen" без plastic-y compound look.
- Result: matte-to-satin finish, organic feel, doesn't reflect light starkly.

### Recommendation

**Path B (soft sheen, burnishing)** — matches Last Night brand identity. Mirror polish looks too "modern" / plastic, breaks ritual aesthetic.

### Tools needed

- Belt sander (для stage 1–2): $60–100.
- Hand sanding blocks (3D-printed or wood, flat surface backing).
- Sandpaper assortment 80 / 120 / 220 / 400 / 600 / 800 / 1000-grit (~$15 total).
- (Optional Path A) Bench grinder + buffing wheel + white rouge: $80.
- (Path B) Burnishing tool — deer antler tip ($5) или smooth river stone (free).

### Time

Per cartridge: **45 minutes – 1 hour** для full sanding sequence + 15 minutes burnishing.

### Common mistakes

- **Thin spots**: too aggressive sanding в одном area → uneven thickness. Calipers check often.
- **Burned spots** (heat от dry sanding): brown/yellow patches. Throw away — bone protein damaged. **Wet sand always.**
- **Linear scratches**: visible at final polish stage если ранее не cross-sanded. Re-sand с 400-grit.
- **Edges chipped**: don't sand edges aggressively — chamfer only ~0.3мм с 220-grit.

## 9. Stabilization — финишная защита

Polished bone is **hygroscopic** — absorbs ambient humidity. Без protection:
- Cracks при humidity changes.
- Bacteria growth (especially если degreasing was incomplete).
- Surface picks up oils от skin contact (yellowing).

But **acoustic application** (Last Night cartridge) — heavy stabilization **kills resonance**.

Balance: light protection, preserve acoustic character.

### Method A — Beeswax (рекомендовано для acoustic use)

**Pros**: light, preserves resonance, food-safe, traditional finish.
**Cons**: needs reapplication every 6–12 months.

**Procedure**:
1. Heat bone plate gently в low oven (40–50°C, 10 minutes) — opens pores slightly.
2. Rub solid beeswax block onto surface — wax melts onto bone.
3. Once cooled (5 minutes): polish с lint-free cotton cloth, hard pressure.
4. Wax penetrates surface, leaves slight sheen.
5. Optional: second coat after 1 hour.

**Cost**: 1 block beeswax ($5) → 50+ cartridges.

### Method B — Mineral oil (lightest)

**Pros**: безопасно, food-grade, very easy.
**Cons**: less protective than wax, requires more frequent reapplication.

**Procedure**:
- Drop of food-grade mineral oil на cloth.
- Rub plate surface.
- Wait 10 minutes for absorption.
- Wipe excess.

Used in cutting boards / wooden utensils — safe для acoustic instrument.

### Method C — Thin CA glue (heaviest, NOT recommended)

**Pros**: maximum hardness, prevents moisture forever.
**Cons**: **kills resonance** — locks bone surface, dampens vibration. Looks plasticy.

Used by woodturners для bone rings, knife handles. **НЕ для acoustic plates**.

If accidentally used: cartridge будет звучать "глушено", less character. Discard.

### Method D — Shellac (compromise)

**Pros**: traditional luthier finish, slightly hardens но preserves vibration.
**Cons**: requires multiple coats, longer cure.

**Procedure**:
- 1:3 shellac flake : alcohol (denatured ethanol).
- Brush thin coat, 12 hour cure.
- Light sand 800-grit.
- Second coat, 12 hour cure.
- Hand-buff с paper bag (final polish).

**Used для violin/guitar finishing** — proven acoustic-friendly.

### Recommendation

**Beeswax (Method A)** для всех Last Night bone cartridges. Simple, effective, preserves character, food-safe для customer handling, traditional aesthetic.

For premium SKU — **shellac (Method D)** — luthier-grade finish, distinguishable от budget tier.

## 10. Acoustic optimization

Bone plate готовка для resonance peak performance:

### Density check

**Cattle scapula cortical bone density**: ~2.0 g/cm³.
For 100×40×4мм plate: **mass ≈ 32г**.

If finished plate weighs:
- **<25г** — too porous, internal voids. Acoustic dull. Reject.
- **25–35г** — acceptable, normal cortical density.
- **>40г** — unusually dense (older animal, hardened). Premium tier — long sustain.

### Tap test

After full processing, hold plate by edge (clamp loosely в vise или fingers), **tap с pencil eraser** на center.

- **Sharp "ping" с sustain 0.5–1с** — excellent. Use it.
- **Dull "thunk", little sustain** — porous, fat-laden, или incomplete drying. Reject или re-process.
- **"Tick" с very long sustain** — exceptional, premium quality. Mark for premium cartridge SKU.

### Mounting position для exciter

Cattle bone has natural fiber/grain orientation. Optimal exciter position:
- **Off-center**, ~1/3 from one edge (avoids node of fundamental mode).
- **Across grain direction**, not parallel (better energy transfer).
- Visual: scapula natural striations visible — mount perpendicular to them.

### Piezo placement

- **Piezo A**: near exciter, 30мм from one edge — captures direct excitation, bright character.
- **Piezo B**: opposite end, 70мм from exciter — captures decay tail, warmer character. Position crossfade controls timbre.

### Expected RT60

For properly processed cattle scapula plate 100×40×4мм:
- **RT60 unforced**: 0.3–0.8с.
- **Character**: dry, ritual, harmonic-rich on transient.
- **Q-factor**: ~80–150 (medium).
- **Self-oscillation threshold**: high feedback — possible but rare. More musical use case.

If actual measurements differ значительно — issue with processing (incomplete drying, residual fat, micro-cracks).

## 11. Safety — техника безопасности

### Health hazards

| Stage | Hazard | PPE |
|-------|--------|-----|
| Sourcing / handling raw | Bacteria (E. coli, Salmonella) на raw meat | Nitrile gloves, hand wash |
| Boiling defleshing | Steam burns, slipping | Apron, splash protection |
| Maceration | Putrescine smell, bacterial culture | Outdoor only, gloves |
| Ammonia degreasing | Respiratory irritant | Respirator (P100 + organic vapor cartridge) |
| Acetone degreasing | Flammable, eye/skin irritant | Goggles, nitrile gloves, no flames |
| Hydrogen peroxide | Skin/eye irritant (12%+) | Goggles, gloves |
| Cutting (wet) | Bone aerosol, sharp edges | N95 mask, eye protection, gloves |
| Cutting (dry — AVOID) | Bone dust silicosis-like | Full P100 respirator + dust collection |
| Sanding | Bone dust | N95 mask, vacuum collector |
| Polishing (buffing wheel) | Dust + flying debris | Face shield, P100 mask |
| Beeswax application | Heat from oven | Heat-resistant gloves |

### Critical "NEVER" list

- **NEVER use chlorine bleach** — destroys bone protein.
- **NEVER cut dry** — fine bone dust is dangerous (silicosis-like illness if chronic exposure).
- **NEVER overheat** (>70°C) — protein denaturation, smoke, brittle bone.
- **NEVER use без ventilation** — fumes harmful in enclosed space.
- **NEVER mix degreasing chemicals** — ammonia + bleach = chloramine gas (toxic).

### Environment

- Bone dust accumulation на shop floor → daily wet-mop.
- Maceration bucket → outdoor, away from neighbours.
- Acetone → flammable cabinet, away from ignition sources.
- Used peroxide → dilute с water, drain disposal OK (decomposes к oxygen + water).
- Bone trim waste → compost or landfill (organic).

### Allergies

Some people allergic к bone dust (similar к feather/keratin allergies). Test small exposure first. If reaction (sneezing, watery eyes) — increase PPE или delegate this work.

## 12. Variants — стилистические варианты

Beyond "ivory white polished":

### Aged / yellowed
Skip whitening step. Keep natural age tone. Looks "vintage".

### Black bone
Soak полированный plate в **India ink** или **leather dye black** для 24h. Penetrates bone surface, deep black finish. Visually strikingly. Used в jewelry / scrimshaw.

### Stained / colored
- **Tea bath**: strong black tea soak 24–48h → warm yellow-brown.
- **Coffee**: similar, slightly redder tone.
- **Walnut hull dye**: deep brown, traditional.
- **Acrylic ink**: any color, less penetrating, more surface coat.

### Carved (scrimshaw)
After polishing, carve patterns с small chisels or rotary tool fine bits. Fill grooves с India ink → dark line drawings on white bone. **Premium SKU** — labor intensive ($50+ retail markup per cartridge).

### Burnished only (raw character)
Skip sandpaper after stage 4 (400-grit). Burnish с antler tip immediately. Surface stays slightly textured, more organic. **Cheapest варианt** — 30 minutes finishing time saved.

### Recommendation для Last Night catalog

- **Standard tier**: ivory natural (peroxide minimal, beeswax). $190 retail.
- **Aged tier**: skip peroxide, sun bleach 1 week. $200 retail (scarcity).
- **Black**: India ink dyed, beeswax. $230 retail (visually striking).
- **Premium scrimshaw**: hand-carved system suicide motif. $400 retail (labor justified).
